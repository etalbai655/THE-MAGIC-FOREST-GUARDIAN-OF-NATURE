THE MAGIC FOREST: GUARDIAN OF NATURE

Science and English Project for 4th Grade Primary Education

5. FINAL PRODUCT

Become a Guardian of Nature!

 
Welcome to our final mission! 🌍✨

In this project, you will work with your team to help save the Magic Forest and protect nature.

Together, you will create a digital environmental campaign to teach other people how to care for the planet.

What will you create? 🤔

🌱 Posters about nature and recycling  
♻️ Eco-friendly ideas to protect animals and forests  
💻 Digital creations using fun online tools  
🗣️ An oral presentation in English  
🤝 Teamwork activities with your classmates  

During the project, you will use different digital tools such as:

🎨 Canva  
📌 Padlet  
🎮 Kahoot  
✨ Genially  

You will investigate, play, create and share your ideas like real nature heroes!

At the end of the project, each team will present their campaign to the class and show how we can all help our planet.

🌟 Are you ready to become a Guardian of Nature? 🌟
